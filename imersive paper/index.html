I. Introduction

The business industry encompasses a wide range of optimization problems, including the classical 0/1 knapsack problem, stock trend prediction [2], wireless sensor network deployment [4], and the traveling salesman problem (TSP). These problems often involve combinatorial decision-making, requiring the selection or exclusion of certain options. Additionally, some problems, such as the TSP, focus not only on which elements to select but also on determining their optimal order — for instance, deciding the sequence in which cities should be visited. Regardless of the specific problem type, the overarching objective is to minimize costs while maximizing overall benefits.

在工商業界會碰到許多最佳化問題，像是無線網路拓樸擺放問題，擇時選股問題、電路合成問題……等。最佳化問題大致有兩種，連續實數的問題，以及離散、選與不選或是排列組合的問題。不論是哪一種，就是要達成成本最小化，利益最大化的目的。

以擇時選股問題為例，在三檔股票時，每檔股票進行選擇，都不選是000，全選是111，有8種組合，複雜度是2的三次方。四檔股票時，有16種組合，複雜度是2的四次方。但是在50檔股票時，複雜度將高達2的50次方，超過一千兆種以上的組合。基於這些最佳化問題過於複雜且困難，無法在短時間內解決。

Metaheuristic algorithms are commonly utilized to handle these complex real-world applications. They may solve non-specific issues through some modification of encoding and achieve a near-optimal solution in a finite amount of time. Most metaheuristic algorithms are population- and iterative-based, and the solution gets better with the evolved process in iterations with efficient and effective searchability, such as the genetic algorithm (GA)[6], particle swarm optimization (PSO)[7], ant colony optimization (ACO)[8], estimation of the distribution algorithm (EDA)[9], quantum-inspired evolutionary algorithm (QEA)[10], and quantum-inspired tabu search algorithm (QTS)[11]. Various optimization problem-solving techniques have their own unique characteristics. Typically, the optimization search process of metaheuristic algorithms used to solve these problems is treated as a black box.

因此就需要最佳化演算法地當忙，常見的像是基因演算法、粒子群優演算法、人工蜂群演算法…..等。在連續實數方面，為了測試演算法的好壞，研究者會使用困難且複雜方程式進行試驗。光是看到這些方程式，就很難想像方程式的長相。就像Schweful、Michalewizs、Ackley更不用說工商業界的方程式都是未知的黑盒子，更加不可能預測他的方程式長相。

以往研究者分析演算法解題過程的時候，會使用命令提示字元、command line、終端機、或DOS模式，也就是俗稱的小黑窗，將過程一一顯示。或是將結果輸出在excel檔上，並對log檔進行分析。

III. Proposed Visualization Tool

Within the domain of business, there are a multitude of optimization challenges that require resolution. Prior to resolving optimization challenges specific to the industry, researchers typically evaluate optimization algorithms using benchmark functions. Enhancing the efficacy of an optimization algorithm on benchmark functions increases the probability that it will successfully tackle optimization functions that are specific to a given industry. In this section, we use three benchmark problems to introduce how the system works on the three interfaces, including GA and QTS solving the 0/1 knapsack problem in the general combinatorial optimization interface, Entanglement-QTS [35] solving the deployment optimization problem in the deployment combinatorial optimization interface, and ACO solving TSP in the permutation/linear combinatorial optimization interface.

General Combinatorial Optimization Interface

In the general combinatorial optimization interface (general interface), all kinds of chosen or not combinatorial optimization problems can be displayed through this interface. Evo-Panel 2.0 can show the fitness, all item combinations in each generation, and items' detailed information so that users can check if the metaheuristic matches their thoughts or problems' constraints.

此外，有的演算法會不只有0與1，選與不選的問題，而是會依據機率來決定，就像這些演算法。點選"機率"按鈕後，使用者可以用紅到藍的漸層顏色來觀察每個粒子的機率。在演變初期，我們可以看見畫面中呈現綠色，代表機率為0.5，要選或不選的機率相同。隨世代演進，我們看見顏色慢慢轉變，機率從0.5漸漸增加到1 或減少到0。越紅的偏選，越藍的偏不選。接著我們可以觀察到在紅色區域中，有幾條藍色的線。發現演算法會自動跳過某些CP值高的物品，改去右邊拿CP值較低的，以滿足背包容量限制，這是0/1背包之所以是NP-Complete問題，也是他極困難且有趣的地方。

選擇台灣地圖后，開始連線地圖右邊會顯示台東連線至高雄的金額並且線條部分也以顏色來區分遠近以方便觀察。當路線選到成本較高，並線條呈現紅色時，重新選擇較短的路線。此外，我們也模擬路況問題例如從南投至花蓮會發生土石流，就像工商業界會遇到許多有限制式，更複雜的最佳化問題，也能增加遊戲的趣味性。玩家連線完畢會顯示遊戲過關。

一旦城市數量變多像是美國和歐洲地圖，此時就會變成複雜度極高的問題，藉助群眾的力量在遊玩的過程中也能幫我們找到最佳解，當越多人玩遊戲，就越多人幫我們做研究。

V. Conclusion

Evo-Panel 2.0 stands as an invaluable and precise tool, providing dynamic visual representations of metaheuristic algorithms solving combinatorial optimization problems. Firstly, it replaces extensive textual data with a visualization interface, streamlining algorithms and optimization problem analyses through vivid graphical depictions. Visualization expedites analytical processes, renders tedious tasks more engaging, deepens user comprehension of metaheuristic concepts and optimization problems, and liberates mental resources for future algorithmic enhancements. Secondly, Evo-Panel 2.0 offers versatile interfaces catering to distinct characteristics of combinatorial optimization problems, including general 'choose or not' interfaces, deployment interfaces for solutions with strong dependencies, and permutation interfaces for sequential solutions. Additionally, it incorporates a probability model tailored for probabilistic metaheuristics. Thirdly, our visualization tool offers interactive features, such as media player control bars for meticulous algorithmic observation and sensor manipulation within the deployment interface to refine metaheuristic solutions. Fourthly, Evo-Panel 2.0's compatibility with all metaheuristics and optimization technologies, sans the need for specific programming knowledge for analysis file generation, underscores its flexibility. Lastly, the visualization tool helps eXplainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) by making it easier to understand how computational intelligence works and revealing hidden insights that can help developers make better, easier to understand metaheuristics that can be used to solve a wide range of business optimization problems.
